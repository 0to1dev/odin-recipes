Bacon and Cheese Omelette Recipe

Description

A Bacon and Cheese Omelette is a simple yet hearty breakfast dish that brings together the smoky flavour of bacon with the creamy richness of melted cheese. The recipe starts with cooking bacon until it's crisp, rendering out its fat, which also adds a deep savoury base. Meanwhile, eggs are beaten (with a bit of salt and pepper) and then poured into a hot pan—often using the bacon fat to cook them for extra flavour. As the eggs begin to set but still have a slightly runny top, shredded cheese is added so it melts into the warm eggs.

Once the cheese is melting and the omelette’s underside has a golden-brown hue, the edges are gently loosened and the omelette is folded, enclosing the cheese and bacon inside. It’s then cooked for a little longer so the inside finishes nicely without becoming dry. When done, this omelette is warm, rich, and satisfying—cheesy, smoky, with the gentle texture contrast between crisp bacon bits and soft eggs. Perfect for a filling breakfast or even a simple dinner.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2-3 strips of bacon
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or your choice)
  • 1 tablespoon butter (optional, for cooking)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon in a skillet over medium heat until crisp, then remove and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, whisk the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. Heat butter (or a bit of the bacon fat) in the skillet, then pour in the eggs.
  4. Let the eggs cook undisturbed until the edges start to set, then sprinkle cheese evenly on top.
  5. Crumble the cooked bacon and add it over the melting cheese.
  6. Fold the omelette in half, cooking for another minute until the cheese is fully melted.
  7. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve warm.
